Clients Question

We understand you might have questions about our programs, services, and equipment. Here are some of the most commonly asked questions to help you get the answers you need quickly.

What kind of equipment do you provide?

We provide all essential hockey gear, including sticks, balls, safety helmets, and goalkeeper equipment. Our equipment meets professional standards to ensure safety and performance.

What does your hockey training include?

Our training programs cover skill development, tactical gameplay, physical fitness, and team-building exercises. We cater to beginners, intermediate, and advanced players.

Can I book private lessons?

Yes, we offer one-on-one private lessons with our expert coaches. These sessions are tailored to focus on specific skills and areas of improvement.

Do you offer ski touring sessions as well?

Although hockey is our main focus, we also provide seasonal ski touring programs led by professional instructors. Contact us for details.

How do I book a program or session?

You can book directly through our website or contact our support team. We recommend early bookings as slots fill up quickly.

Where can I find pricing details?

You can find all pricing information on our website under the 'Programs' or 'Services' sections. For customized packages, please contact us directly.
